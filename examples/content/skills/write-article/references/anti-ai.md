# Anti-AI Checklist for Zhihu Articles

When reviewing a draft, scan for these AI tells and remove them.

## Banned phrases

- "这不仅仅是A，更是B"
- "某种意义上来说"
- "X 是一种强大的工具"
- "随着 X 的发展"
- "在这个快速变化的时代"
- "总之 / 综上 / 最后"

## Structural smells

- Perfectly symmetric sections (intro / 3 reasons / conclusion)
- Every paragraph the same length
- No tangents, no asides, no personal voice
- Bulleted list when prose would be more natural

## Content smells

- Generic examples ("假设有一个用户...")
- No specific numbers, dates, or names
- No mention of pitfalls or things that went wrong
- Conclusions that "balance both sides" instead of taking a position

## Tone fixes

- Add one line of self-deprecation per 1000 words
- Include at least one swear-equivalent ("这玩意儿真垃圾", "我当时蒙了")
- One tangent that loops back is better than three on-topic paragraphs
